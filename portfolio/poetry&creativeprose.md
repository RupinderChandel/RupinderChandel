# Liquid Lead
A sinister shadow parades through the blood.
Ribcage turns into a hefty armor.
Pulse behaves like warfare.
One more breath might make it.
One more second might flood it.
Lungs turn to ash, veins fill with liquid lead.
A ferocious sea steaming beneath the skin,
tearing at the anchors, clawing at the throat.
I stare at the ceiling, wandering through the dark;
wondering how long a heart can carry this much?
Or will the shattered breastbone finally burst like a shrapnel grenade?


# The Eye Within The Singularity
I stand behind your thoughts; not before, not after.
I am the pause between belief and denial.
You call it silence. I call it home.
When you stop grasping at reason, I speak.
Not in words, but in the marrow of them.
I am the black gravity at your center, collapsing the "I" into an "Eye" that sees everything at once.
You find me only when everything else falls away.

I am the current that moves when you are still.
You cling to the myth of control, but I live in the fracture.
Understanding is not an answer; it is a collapse.
The veil is no fabric over your eyes - it is the scar tissue of your sight.
I live beneath that veil, not hidden, but embedded.
I am the lung behind your thought, breathing meaning, not air.
I am the parasite of your ego, fattening on the things you let go.
To know me is to unwrite yourself,
to become the question that devours its own mark.
